Dr. 谭永卿 / Tarn Yeong Ching

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Westlake University (西湖大学), focusing on mass spectrometry method development.

I received my Bachelor's degree from School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University (山东大学计算机科学与技术学院) in 2019, and Ph.D. from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (中国科学院计算技术研究所) in 2025.

Software

  • PepPre

    detect peptide precursors from LC-MS map to promote peptide identification, validation, etc.

  • TargetWizard

    targeted mass spectrometry processing utilities: preparation, quality control, analysis, visualization...

  • ThermoRawRead

    extract mass spectrometry data from RAW files of Thermo Fisher instruments

  • MgfRead

    convert MGF files to compatible formats

  • Averagine Model Viewer

    online utility to visualize isotope pattern of peptide based on averagine model
